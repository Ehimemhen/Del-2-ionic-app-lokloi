M-Know Malaria App (know about malaria), is a handy informative tool set packaged in a mobile application for different platforms to help fight against malaria.

This App is designed to provide assistance and support to inhabitants living in malaria endemic regions and to people traveling to and from malaria endemic zones across the world.

M-Know Malaria App would be a free service to all especially to population at risk of malaria..
